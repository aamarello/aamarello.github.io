Who I Am

This image is of great importance to me. I made it
back when I was going to therapy. My therapist wanted
me to write what my perception of myself was and
I felt a drawing would be better. Theres a lot of
symbolism in this. It makes me feel mixed emotions.
It makes me feel happy because its an image that
shows me when I'm not hiding behind a mask per say.
It makes me feel depressed at the same time because
I made this during the most emotionally straining
time of my life. Its a bitter swwet image that I'll
always be happy that I made.
