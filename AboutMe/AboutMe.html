I was born August 14th 1997. Ive always liked art however when I was younger I would often flip flop between wanting to be a writer and wanting to be an artist. Eventually in college I made the decision to pursue art. My main goal as an artist is to inspire feelings of hope, love, and an appreciation for the little things in life. I want my viewers to feel that yes life is hard and outright unbearable at points, but never give up because theres so much in this world to feel happy for! and there's plenty of things worth fighting for!
