Value of a Dollar

For this assignment we had to take 50 items that
were a dollar or less and categorize them seven different
ways. I like pasta so i took 50 different types of pasta
and catagorized them by price, shape, container type,
Whether or not they come with sauce, Whether or not they
are microwavable, Which ones I've had, and what store
they came from. It was a long process but it was
worth it!
